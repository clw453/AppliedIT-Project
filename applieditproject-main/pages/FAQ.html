The Colorado River is a truly wonderful rafting experience that's perfect for all ages (3+) close to Steamboat Springs, CO. We meet you on the river 1 hour South of Steamboat Springs. This rafting experience is the real deal, the Colorado River is over 1,450 miles long, and will eventually carve out the Grand Canyon, and encompasses some of the most mysteriously wild and unexplored areas in the world.
Depending on flows, our half-day trips will cover between 5-10 miles. Give us a call and ask us how many miles you'll be floating on your trip.
The Colorado River and the Eagle River offer the closest rafting to the Vail area. We meet trips on the Colorado River about 45 minutes Northwest of Vail. We meet Eagle river trips about 20 minutes West of Vail.

Colorado River

Splash into the Colorado River in a raft or inflatable kayak! The Colorado River offers something for everyone, as a fun, splashy, and beautiful beginner whitewater rafting experience close to both Steamboat Springs and Vail. The Colorado river is perfect for the whole family. Come experience a scenic tour through the headwaters of the Colorado River today! We offer half day and full day depatures daily. Class I-III

Eagle River

The Eagle River is one of Colorado's last truly untamed & undammed rivers in the state. The Eagle River's snow-melt rapids make for an exciting once in a lifetime experience. Continuous whitewater & adrenaline pumping rapids makes the Eagle River an adventure you shouldn't miss. Half day and full day departures daily dependent on flows (May-June/July). Class I-IV


Book Now
