Colorado River Guides

Colorado River Guides is a small, family owned and operated rafting company near Steamboat Springs & Vail Colorado, we take great pride in your safety and enjoyment of our rafting trips. We specialize in families, groups, teams, and other customized rafting trips from Steamboat Springs, Vail, and surrounding areas. Our company values are just two of the reasons guests return and refer us to their friends and family.
Colorado River Guides has over 30 years of experience, bringing visitors and locals alike to some of the best waterways in Northwestern Colorado. Our rafting trips, enjoyed best in our large rafts and inflatable kayaks, take you through some of America's most beautiful country - towering canyon walls, exotic red rock territory, sandy beaches and lush riverside vegetation.

Colorado River

Splash into the Colorado River in a raft or inflatable kayak! The Colorado River offers something for everyone, as a fun, splashy, and beautiful beginner whitewater rafting experience close to both Steamboat Springs and Vail. The Colorado river is perfect for the whole family. Come experience a scenic tour through the headwaters of the Colorado River today! We offer half day and full day depatures daily. Class I-III

Eagle River

The Eagle River is one of Colorado's last truly untamed & undammed rivers in the state. The Eagle River's snow-melt rapids make for an exciting once in a lifetime experience. Continuous whitewater & adrenaline pumping rapids makes the Eagle River an adventure you shouldn't miss. Half day and full day departures daily dependent on flows (May-June/July). Class I-IV
